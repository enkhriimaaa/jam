Biography

Lady Gaga - Biography

Early Life

Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta, known professionally as Lady Gaga, was born on March 28, 1986, in New York City. She began playing the piano at age four and started composing music at 13. Gaga attended the Tisch School of the Arts at NYU before dropping out to pursue a music career.

Musical Breakthrough

In 2008, Lady Gaga released her debut album, The Fame, which included hits like "Just Dance" and "Poker Face." The album's success catapulted her to international stardom. She followed up with The Fame Monster in 2009, featuring the hit single "Bad Romance."

Acting Career

Gaga expanded her talents into acting, earning critical acclaim for her role in the 2018 film A Star Is Born, where she starred alongside Bradley Cooper. Her performance earned her an Academy Award nomination and a Grammy Award for the song "Shallow."

Recent Work

In 2025, Lady Gaga released her seventh studio album, Mayhem, which features the hit single "Die with a Smile" with Bruno Mars. The album showcases her return to her pop roots and has received critical acclaim.
